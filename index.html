Artist Statement

My digital art focuses on gaming, the characters from those games, and my experiences in life. My work does incoporate a lot of elements from games like Halo, Gears of War, and Call of Duty. Even other artworks I use other games I play as the main subject when I make an artwork. Photoshop and Illustrator are my main media that I would use as an artist, but I have dabbled in Photography, Video, and 3D. When it comes to video games I have a lot to tell to people because it has been with me most of my life. What I think that an artist should display in their artworks is their experiences and what they are passionate about. In my case its video games, I'm passionate about video games and my love for Xbox. I have widen my scope of what media I can use and it makes an versatile artist.
