Glitch Art and Mashup
This artwork is an digital collage featuring video game characters that include Master Chief, Marcus Fenix, and an military soldier from the game Call of Duty, rendered with glitch effects, static interference, and dynamic color distortion. This is an Mashup and Glitch Art combination where we can see what is distorted by changing the code of the image.

Loneliness Emotion Group Project
This is digital art piece created in Blender where my group picked an emotion and in Blender we would create the emotion as a 3D object to represent the emotion we picked. We picked loneliness and we made person in a broken house isolated by themselves currled up in a ball on a chair. This is total loneliness and expresses the emotion of being alone.
